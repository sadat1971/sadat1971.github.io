The problem

Families abroad struggle to coordinate trusted care for parents back home.

01

Distance creates stress

Overseas children cannot physically manage appointments and hospital logistics.

02

Care is fragmented

Transport, tests, prescriptions, reports, and follow-ups are manually coordinated.

03

Trust is unclear

No curated platform exists for selecting reliable elderly support helpers.

04

Supply is unstructured

Potential caregivers exist, but screening, training, and ratings are missing.

Our solution

A curated platform connecting expatriate families, trained caregivers, clinics, diagnostics, and training partners.

Families book and monitor care services while verified local providers earn through structured, accountable work.
